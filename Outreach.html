Outreach

Outreach is an essential part of being a FIRST team. Through outreach efforts, teams can inspire and engage young students to pursue interests in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM).

By visiting schools, hosting workshops, and participating in community events, FIRST team members serve as ambassadors who demonstrate the excitement of hands-on learning and robotics. Outreach allows teams to give back to their local community by sparking curiosity in STEM fields and the FIRST program. It provides opportunities for students to develop leadership, communication, and public speaking skills. Outreach is also a way for teams to raise awareness about their team's activities, attract new team members and mentors, and generate support from sponsors. Ultimately, outreach amplifies the impact of the FIRST program by spreading its mission of inspiring students in STEM.
